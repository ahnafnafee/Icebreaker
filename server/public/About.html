About


IceBreaker is a dating application designed to connect people from all backgrounds.
Our goal is to connect people not through the traditional methods of posting photos of your beauty and adventures, but through encouraging the display of more creative media, which demonstrates various things such as what your hobbies are, your personal outlook on life, or even what kind of beverage you enjoy.

Through this method, we hope users will get more of a glimpse into an individual's personality and lifestyle, and find new ways to connect and start a conversation.
